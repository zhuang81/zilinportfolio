For the blue butterfly pattern, I started off with a reference photo of a chinese knot and took inspirations from there. Since the knot was red, I wanted to use a differnt color so the design doesn't resemble a chinese knot. I picked three colors from the monochromatic pallet then spinned the design around for it to resembele a butterfly. Lastly, I used the grid pattern and adjusted it so fits the background.

For the green square pattern, I also started off with a volleyball as a reference. I wanted this pattern to be minimalistic and give a calm, balanced mood. So, I chose to use rectangles to create that minimalistic vibe and used color green to create a calm, peaceful mood.
