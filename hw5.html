I have the text as a layer and the shapes on another layer. For the text, I use the pencil tool to draw paths. On the word "Broken", I made the letters look fractured to match with the meaning of the word and contrats with the background. And I also made the word "Bridge" look round off to contrast with its background. On the shape layer, I decided to use rectangles and cirles becuase they can represent money (this piece argues about economic division). I colored some shapes green and yellow to represent wealth and places them behind the text "Broken". Then i colored some shapes brown and dull red to represnet proverty, and places them behind the text "Bridge". This is meant to contrast with the text but complements with the background. The star shape meant to represent hope for reconnection of the two economic groups.
